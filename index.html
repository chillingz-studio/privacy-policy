Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

ChillingZ ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free mobile puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the app.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game data (such as your high score, settings, and theme preference) is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server.

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage and ad interaction data
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to personally identifiable information collected by AdMob.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob — Ad serving and monetization
    https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal risk to your information. Game data stored on your device is subject to your device's own security measures.

Your Choices

You can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app with an updated "Last updated" date.
